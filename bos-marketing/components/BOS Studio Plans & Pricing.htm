Frequently asked questions
Is pricing per user or per seat? +
No. BOS does not charge per user. Pricing is per business you activate capabilities and govern usage. Headcount doesn't drive your bill.
What happens when I hit a usage limit? +
Execution pauses and admins are notified. There are no automatic overages or surprise charges. You decide whether to upgrade it's a feature, not a bug.
What are FX modules? +
FX modules are capability activations Avatar FX, Concierge FX, and DevOps Copilot FX each unlock a specific layer of intelligence inside BOS. Chat FX and Project FX are always included. Each module has a flat annual activation fee and operates under governed usage tiers.
What is BOS MBA? +
BOS MBA is our Early Partner Program a limited founding cohort for businesses ready to deploy BOS and help shape the platform. BOS MBA is a selective enrollment program for founding partners. You get hands-on deployment inside your business, direct roadmap input, and cohort access alongside other founders and operators. Enrollment is by application only.
What is iWorkspace? +
iWorkspace is BOS's AI-powered intelligence layer for research, brainstorming, and execution built directly into the platform. Instead of switching to a separate AI tool, your team runs deep research, ideation sessions, and analytical work inside the same environment where work gets done and executed.
How does BOS compare to paying for Slack + AI tools separately? +
A 25-person team paying for Slack, Google Workspace, and layered AI tools easily spends $1,000–$2,500/month before adding any automation. BOS Growth overrules all of that communication, project management, agents, storage, and an AI intelligence layer under one business license.
